The Legacy of Nikola Tesla

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine." - Nikola Tesla

Tesla's Impact on Modern Technology

  • Wireless Technology

    Tesla's work on wireless power transmission laid the foundation for modern wireless communication technologies.

  • Electric Power Distribution

    The AC power system developed by Tesla is the standard for power distribution worldwide.

  • Electric Motors

    Tesla's induction motor design is still used in many industrial applications and electric vehicles.

  • Renewable Energy

    Tesla's vision of free, abundant energy aligns with modern efforts in renewable energy development.
