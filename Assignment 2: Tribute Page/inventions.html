Nikola Tesla's Inventions

Tesla Coil

A high-voltage electrical transformer circuit used to produce high-voltage, low-current, high-frequency alternating-current electricity.

Alternating Current (AC) System

Tesla's most famous invention, the AC electrical system, powers homes and buildings today.

Induction Motor

An AC electric motor that revolutionized the manufacturing and transportation industries.

Tesla Turbine

A bladeless centripetal flow turbine patented by Tesla in 1913.
